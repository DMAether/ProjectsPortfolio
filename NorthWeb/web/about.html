Who we are

We are a company that stock and deliver a variety of tools, equipment, and chemicals for various gemology practices. We are focused on giving our clients a wide range of supplies and products at a fair price with timely delivery.

How we work

Our website contains a catalogue of all available items for order, however, prices are on a by-quote basis. Once your cart is completed, the order will be sent to our staff, who will then contact you with a quote for your selected items. Once payment is made, we will begin shipping your order

Deliveries

All deliveries will be subject to a delivery fee, which is calculated along with the quote. Deliveries are either done by employees or through a courier service. Deliveries are limited to Johannesburg
